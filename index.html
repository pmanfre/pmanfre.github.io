About

This website serves as a portfolio for the work done by Peyton Manfre and peers in a Writing and Digital Media course at Virginia Tech. The projects, including two blogposts, a podcast, and a video all center around the arts and serve to showcase the value all arts hold even in an environment centered around science and innovation.
