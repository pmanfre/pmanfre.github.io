About

English and Public Relations double major at Virginia Tech, Peyton Manfre, used the projects in this Writing and Digital Media course as a way to showcase her passion for music and film which often reside outside the realm of what is an "acceptable career path" for students at Tech. While there are many liberal arts students at the university, the school has a reputation mainly for its engineering and science based programs. This website serves to showcase that anyone anywhere can have passion for the arts.
